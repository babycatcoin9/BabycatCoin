BabyCat Coin

BabyCat Coin is a unique new meme-based token with a goal-oriented and detailed roadmap to take over the meme coins world. BabyCat Coin is a deflationary and revolutionary meme token, community-oriented, with locked LP, low slippage, fully renounced token ownership and meme-driven. BabyCat is an Arbitrum token, fully audited and posterity-focused.

The vision of BabyCat Coin is aimed at creating BabyCat AI NFT trait. These NFTs are rare breeds of digital cats that will resonate with real-time cats and owned by users as pet. BabyCat Coin holders will be rewarded with BabyCat NFT, such that users can own, purchase and/or sell on a p2p platform.

Features

Fair Launch

With no preference or luck whatsoever, BabyCat Coin is available for everyone on Sushiswap.

Liquidity

BabyCat Coin LP is currently totally locked ad infinitum. All contract ownership and access has been publicly renounced, as a community driven project we promote the decentralization of cryptocurrency.

Community Oriented

BabyCat Coin is not focused on the numerical strength of community but on the uniqueness and engagement of our community. We strive to build an organic, real and engaging community for greater reward.

Low Slippage

High ‘buy and sell tax’ is a recurring challenge for token acceptance and ownership. Thus, BabyCat Coin has a low slippage to ease the withdrawal and deposit of BabyCat Coin for our community.
